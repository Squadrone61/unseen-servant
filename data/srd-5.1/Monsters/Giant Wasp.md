_Medium beast, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 12
- **Hit Points** 13 (3d8)
- **Speed** 10 ft., fly 50 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     10 |     14 |     10 |      1 |     10 |      3 |
| **Modifier**     |     +0 |     +2 |     +0 |     -5 |     +0 |     -4 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +0 |     +2 |     +0 |     -5 |     +0 |     -4 |

- **Senses** passive Perception 10
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1/2 (100 XP)

## Actions

_**Sting.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. _Hit:_ 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 11 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If the poison damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, the target is stable but poisoned for 1 hour, even after regaining hit points, and is paralyzed while poisoned in this way.
