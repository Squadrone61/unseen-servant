# Giant Badger

_Medium beast, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 10
- **Hit Points** 13 (2d8 + 4)
- **Speed** 30 ft., burrow 10 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     13 |     10 |     15 |      2 |     12 |      5 |
| **Modifier**     |     +1 |     +0 |     +2 |     -4 |     +1 |     -3 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +1 |     +0 |     +2 |     -4 |     +1 |     -3 |

- **Senses** darkvision 30 ft., passive Perception 11
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1/4 (50 XP)

## Traits

_**Keen Smell.**_ The badger has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The badger makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 4 (1d6 + 1) piercing damage.

_**Claws.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 6 (2d4 + 1) slashing damage.
