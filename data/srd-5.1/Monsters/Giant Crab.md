# Giant Crab

_Medium beast, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 13 (3d8)
- **Speed** 30 ft., swim 30 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     13 |     15 |     11 |      1 |      9 |      3 |
| **Modifier**     |     +1 |     +2 |     +0 |     -5 |     -1 |     -4 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +1 |     +2 |     +0 |     -5 |     -1 |     -4 |

- **Skills** Stealth +4
- **Senses** blindsight 30 ft., passive Perception 9
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1/8 (25 XP)

## Traits

_**Amphibious.**_ The crab can breathe air and water.

## Actions

_**Claw.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 4 (1d6 + 1) bludgeoning damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 11). The crab has two claws, each of which can grapple only one target.
