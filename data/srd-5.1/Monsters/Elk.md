# Elk

_Large beast, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 10
- **Hit Points** 13 (2d10 + 2)
- **Speed** 50 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     16 |     10 |     12 |      2 |     10 |      6 |
| **Modifier**     |     +3 |     +0 |     +1 |     -4 |     +0 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +3 |     +0 |     +1 |     -4 |     +0 |     -2 |

- **Senses** passive Perception 10
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1/4 (50 XP)

## Traits

_**Charge.**_ If the elk moves at least 20 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a ram attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 7 (2d6) damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

## Actions

_**Ram.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage.

_**Hooves.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one prone creature. _Hit:_ 8 (2d4 + 3) bludgeoning damage.
