# Awakened Shrub

_Small plant, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 9
- **Hit Points** 10 (3d6)
- **Speed** 20 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |      3 |      8 |     11 |     10 |     10 |      6 |
| **Modifier**     |     -4 |     -1 |     +0 |     +0 |     +0 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     -4 |     -1 |     +0 |     +0 |     +0 |     -2 |

- **Damage Vulnerabilities** fire
- **Damage Resistances** piercing
- **Senses** passive Perception 10
- **Languages** one language known by its creator
- **Challenge** 0 (10 XP)

## Traits

_**False Appearance.**_ While the shrub remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a normal shrub.

## Actions

_**Rake.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 1 (1d4 - 1) slashing damage.

An **awakened shrub** is an ordinary shrub given sentience and mobility by the _Awaken_ spell or similar magic.
