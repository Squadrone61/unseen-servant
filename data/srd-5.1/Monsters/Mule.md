# Mule

_Medium beast, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 10
- **Hit Points** 11 (2d8 + 2)
- **Speed** 40 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     14 |     10 |     13 |      2 |     10 |      5 |
| **Modifier**     |     +2 |     +0 |     +1 |     -4 |     +0 |     -3 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +2 |     +0 |     +1 |     -4 |     +0 |     -3 |

- **Senses** passive Perception 10
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1/8 (25 XP)

## Traits

_**Beast of Burden.**_ The mule is considered to be a Large animal for the purpose of determining its carrying capacity.

_**Sure-Footed.**_ The mule has advantage on Strength and Dexterity saving throws made against effects that would knock it prone.

## Actions

_**Hooves.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 4 (1d4 + 2) bludgeoning damage.
