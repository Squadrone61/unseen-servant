# Hippogriff

_Large monstrosity, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 11
- **Hit Points** 19 (3d10 + 3)
- **Speed** 40 ft., fly 60 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     17 |     13 |     13 |      2 |     12 |      8 |
| **Modifier**     |     +3 |     +1 |     +1 |     -4 |     +1 |     -1 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +3 |     +1 |     +1 |     -4 |     +1 |     -1 |

- **Skills** Perception +5
- **Senses** passive Perception 15
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1 (200 XP)

## Traits

_**Keen Sight.**_ The hippogriff has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The hippogriff makes two attacks: one with its beak and one with its claws.

_**Beak.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 8 (1d10 + 3) piercing damage.

_**Claws.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 10 (2d6 + 3) slashing damage.
