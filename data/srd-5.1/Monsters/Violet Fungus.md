# Violet Fungus

_Medium plant, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 5
- **Hit Points** 18 (4d8)
- **Speed** 5 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |      3 |      1 |     10 |      1 |      3 |      1 |
| **Modifier**     |     -4 |     -5 |     +0 |     -5 |     -4 |     -5 |
| **Saving Throw** |     -4 |     -5 |     +0 |     -5 |     -4 |     -5 |

- **Condition Immunities** blinded, deafened, frightened
- **Senses** blindsight 30 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive Perception 6
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1/4 (50 XP)

## Traits

_**False Appearance.**_ While the violet fungus remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from an ordinary fungus.

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The fungus makes 1d4 Rotting Touch attacks.

_**Rotting Touch.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +2 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature. _Hit:_ 4 (1d8) necrotic damage.
