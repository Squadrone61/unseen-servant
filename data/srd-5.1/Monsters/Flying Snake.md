# Flying Snake

_Tiny beast, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 14
- **Hit Points** 5 (2d4)
- **Speed** 30 ft., fly 60 ft., swim 30 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |      4 |     18 |     11 |      2 |     12 |      5 |
| **Modifier**     |     -3 |     +4 |     +0 |     -4 |     +1 |     -3 |
| **Saving Throw** |     -3 |     +4 |     +0 |     -4 |     +1 |     -3 |

- **Senses** blindsight 10 ft., passive Perception 11
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1/8 (25 XP)

## Traits

_**Flyby.**_ The snake doesn't provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of an enemy's reach.

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 1 piercing damage plus 7 (3d4) poison damage.

A **flying snake** is a brightly colored, winged serpent found in remote jungles. Tribespeople and cultists sometimes domesticate flying snakes to serve as messengers that deliver scrolls wrapped in their coils.
