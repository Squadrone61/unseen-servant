# Black Bear

_Medium beast, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 11 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 19 (3d8 + 6)
- **Speed** 40 ft., climb 30 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     15 |     10 |     14 |      2 |     12 |      7 |
| **Modifier**     |     +2 |     +0 |     +2 |     -4 |     +1 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +2 |     +0 |     +2 |     -4 |     +1 |     -2 |

- **Skills** Perception +3
- **Senses** passive Perception 13
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1/2 (100 XP)

## Traits

_**Keen Smell.**_ The bear has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The bear makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

_**Claws.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 7 (2d4 + 2) slashing damage.
