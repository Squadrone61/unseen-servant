# Killer Whale

_Huge beast, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 12 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 90 (12d12 + 12)
- **Speed** 0 ft., swim 60 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     19 |     10 |     13 |      3 |     12 |      7 |
| **Modifier**     |     +4 |     +0 |     +1 |     -4 |     +1 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +4 |     +0 |     +1 |     -4 |     +1 |     -2 |

- **Skills** Perception +3
- **Senses** blindsight 120 ft., passive Perception 13
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 3 (700 XP)

## Traits

_**Echolocation.**_ The whale can't use its blindsight while deafened.

_**Hold Breath.**_ The whale can hold its breath for 30 minutes.

_**Keen Hearing.**_ The whale has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing.

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 21 (5d6 + 4) piercing damage.
