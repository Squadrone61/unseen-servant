# Swarm of Ravens

_Medium swarm of Tiny beasts, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 12
- **Hit Points** 24 (7d8 - 7)
- **Speed** 10 ft., fly 50 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |      6 |     14 |      8 |      3 |     12 |      6 |
| **Modifier**     |     -2 |     +2 |     -1 |     -4 |     +1 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     -2 |     +2 |     -1 |     -4 |     +1 |     -2 |

- **Skills** Perception +5
- **Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, slashing
- **Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, prone, restrained, stunned
- **Senses** passive Perception 15
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1/4 (50 XP)

## Traits

_**Swarm.**_ The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny raven. The swarm can't regain hit points or gain temporary hit points.

## Actions

_**Beaks.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target in the swarm's space. _Hit:_ 7 (2d6) piercing damage, or 3 (1d6) piercing damage if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer.
