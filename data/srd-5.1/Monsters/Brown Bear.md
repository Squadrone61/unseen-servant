# Brown Bear

_Large beast, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 11 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 34 (4d10 + 12)
- **Speed** 40 ft., climb 30 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     19 |     10 |     16 |      2 |     13 |      7 |
| **Modifier**     |     +4 |     +0 |     +3 |     -4 |     +1 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +4 |     +0 |     +3 |     -4 |     +1 |     -2 |

- **Skills** Perception +3
- **Senses** passive Perception 13
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1 (200 XP)

## Traits

_**Keen Smell.**_ The bear has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The bear makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing damage.

_**Claws.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.
