# Hill Giant

_Huge giant, chaotic evil_

- **Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 105 (10d12 + 40)
- **Speed** 40 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     21 |      8 |     19 |      5 |      9 |      6 |
| **Modifier**     |     +5 |     -1 |     +4 |     -3 |     -1 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +5 |     -1 |     +4 |     -3 |     -1 |     -2 |

- **Skills** Perception +2
- **Senses** passive Perception 12
- **Languages** Giant
- **Challenge** 5 (1,800 XP)

## Actions

_**Multiattack.**_ The giant makes two greatclub attacks.

_**Greatclub.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 18 (3d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage.

_**Rock.**_ _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, range 60/240 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 21 (3d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage.
