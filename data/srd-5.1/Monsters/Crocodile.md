# Crocodile

_Large beast, unaligned_

- **Armor Class** 12 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 19 (3d10 + 3)
- **Speed** 20 ft., swim 30 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     15 |     10 |     13 |      2 |     10 |      5 |
| **Modifier**     |     +2 |     +0 |     +1 |     -4 |     +0 |     -3 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +2 |     +0 |     +1 |     -4 |     +0 |     -3 |

- **Skills** Stealth +2
- **Senses** passive Perception 10
- **Languages** —
- **Challenge** 1/2 (100 XP)

## Traits

_**Hold Breath.**_ The crocodile can hold its breath for 15 minutes.

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. _Hit:_ 7 (1d10 + 2) piercing damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 12). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the crocodile can't bite another target.
