# Homunculus

_Tiny construct, neutral_

- **Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points** 5 (2d4)
- **Speed** 20 ft., fly 40 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |      4 |     15 |     11 |     10 |     10 |      7 |
| **Modifier**     |     -3 |     +2 |     +0 |     +0 |     +0 |     -2 |
| **Saving Throw** |     -3 |     +2 |     +0 |     +0 |     +0 |     -2 |

- **Damage Immunities** poison
- **Condition Immunities** charmed, poisoned
- **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
- **Languages** understands the languages of its creator but can't speak
- **Challenge** 0 (10 XP)

## Traits

_**Telepathic Bond.**_ While the homunculus is on the same plane of existence as its master, it can magically convey what it senses to its master, and the two can communicate telepathically.

## Actions

_**Bite.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. _Hit:_ 1 piercing damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 minute. If the saving throw fails by 5 or more, the target is instead poisoned for 5 (1d10) minutes and unconscious while poisoned in this way.
