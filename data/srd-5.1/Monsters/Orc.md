# Orc

_Medium humanoid (orc), chaotic evil_

- **Armor Class** 13 (hide armor)
- **Hit Points** 15 (2d8 + 6)
- **Speed** 30 ft.

|                  |   Str. |   Dex. |   Con. |   Int. |   Wis. |   Cha. |
|------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Score**        |     16 |     12 |     16 |      7 |     11 |     10 |
| **Modifier**     |     +3 |     +1 |     +3 |     -2 |     +0 |     +0 |
| **Saving Throw** |     +3 |     +1 |     +3 |     -2 |     +0 |     +0 |

- **Skills** Intimidation +2
- **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
- **Languages** Common, Orc
- **Challenge** 1/2 (100 XP)

## Traits

_**Aggressive.**_ As a bonus action, the orc can move up to its speed toward a hostile creature that it can see.

## Actions

_**Greataxe.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 9 (1d12 + 3) slashing damage.

_**Javelin.**_ _Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage.
